Why use WhisperDesk?

We built this tool because uploading sensitive meeting recordings to the cloud is risky. WhisperDesk runs entirely on your hardware.

100% Private

Your audio never leaves your device. Transcription happens locally using your CPU or GPU.

GPU Accelerated

Optimized for Apple Silicon (Metal) and NVIDIA (CUDA) for blazing fast 100x real-time speeds.

Multi-Format Export

Export to SRT, VTT, JSON, or plain text. Perfect for subtitles, content creation, or archives.

Open Source & Community Driven

WhisperDesk is free software licensed under MIT. Transparency is key when handling private data. Check our code, contribute features, or build your own fork.
